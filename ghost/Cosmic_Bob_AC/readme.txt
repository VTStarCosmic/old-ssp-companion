--Animal Crossing: Bob, README--

Thanks for downloading Bob. :) He may be a bit lazy, but he has a good heart...and snacks! Bob is perfectly content to sit and do nothing. If you want to talk with him, you can double-click him anywhere to bring up his dialogue menu. There should be keybinds for him to chat casually ("t"), or repeat his last dialogue ("r"). You can view his personal menu with right-click, and maybe even pet him on the head by hovering with your mouse.

If you need to contact me for any reason (or would just like to see my work), you can find me as 'Agt Cosmic' anywhere I have an account, including deviantArt, AO3, Twitter, Discord...all that good stuff. I'm always open to bug reporting so I can improve my code!
